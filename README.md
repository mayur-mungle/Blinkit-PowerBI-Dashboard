# 🛒 Blinkit-PowerBI-Dashboard
Power BI dashboard built on Blinkit grocery sales data to analyze outlet performance, item sales, and regional trends.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop

---

## 📊 KPIs
- Total Sales — $1.20M
- Avg Sales — $141
- No of Items — 8523
- Avg Rating — 3.9

---

## 📈 Charts
- Sales by Item Fat Content
- Sales by Outlet Establishment Year
- Sales by Item Type
- Sales by Outlet Size
- No of Items by Outlet Type
- Sales by Outlet Location Type

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods are the top selling categories
- Tier 3 locations generate the highest sales (472.13K)
- 2018 recorded the highest sales peak of 205K
- Medium sized outlets lead in total sales (507.9K)
